User Profile for Medical Lab Results

Description

The dashboard is designed to help patients easily and effectively review and understand their medical lab results. It allows users to input information about their health status, facilitating the monitoring and management of their condition.

Objective

The main objective of the project is to provide an accessible tool for patients to review the results of their medical labs, thereby improving medical care and prevention.

Features

  • Clear labels that indicate the level of attention needed for each result.
  • AI generated insights based on user data.
  • Option to add additional family members.
  • Overview of their order history and lab results.

Persona

User Story

Frustrations

- Hates having to wait to have her lab results explained to her.
- Feels overwhelmed by the amount of information provided.
- Worries about the accuracy of her self-reported data.

Goals

- Wants to have a clear understanding of her lab results.
- Desires a more proactive role in managing her health.
- Hopes to have a tool that helps her track her health over time.

Research

To understand the needs and challenges faced by the main users of the medical labs service I conducted user interviews in order to better understand and empathize with the user, their needs, and pain points. I also analyzed the competitive landscape to identify gaps and opportunities for improvement.

Key Findings

  • Users often feel overwhelmed by the amount of information provided in their lab results.
  • Many users express a desire for a more proactive role in managing their health.
  • There is a need for clear labels that indicate the level of attention needed for each result.
  • Users are interested in having AI-generated insights based on their data.
