Redesign of an app for real estate assessments

Description

It is a web-based application used to manage files, communication, and the status of notarial assessments for real estate properties.

Objective

The application is designed to help users easily and effectively manage their files and communication with notaries. It allows users to input information about their properties, facilitating the monitoring and management of their assessments.

Features

  • File management
  • Communication with notaries
  • Property status tracking
  • Document upload and download
  • User-friendly interface

Persona

User Story

Tere is a 40-year-old real estate agent who works in Mexico City. She has a bachelor's degree in Business Administration and has been working in the real estate sector for 15 years. Tere is married and has two children. She is tech-savvy and uses her smartphone and computer daily to manage her work. Tere is looking for a web-based application that allows her to manage files, communicate with notaries, and track the status of her real estate assessments efficiently.

Age: 40 years

Frustrations

- Difficulty in managing files and communication with notaries.
- Lack of a centralized platform to track the status of assessments.
- Inefficient document management process.
- Difficulty in accessing and sharing files with clients and notaries.

Goals

- Find a web-based application that allows her to manage files and communication with notaries efficiently.
- Track the status of her real estate assessments in real-time.
- Improve the document management process to save time and effort.
- Access and share files easily with clients and notaries.
