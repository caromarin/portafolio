Web design for real estate services

Description

This is a website for a network of real estate brokers to whowcase services and properties that are offered to clients. The website is designed to be user-friendly and visually appealing, with a focus on showcasing the services offered by the network of brokers.

Objective

The website is design for two types of users, the real estate brokers who might want to join the network and the buyers/renters looking for a property.

Features

  • Property listings
  • Broker profiles
  • Contact forms
  • Search functionality
  • Responsive design

Persona

User Story

As a real estate broker I want to showcase the properties and services I offer so that potential clients can find me and contact me easily without having to take care of the technical aspects of the website.

Age: 30 years
Gender: Male
Education: Bachelor's degree in Business Administration
Location: Mexico City
Marital Status: Single
Occupation: Real Estate Agent

Frustrations

- Difficulty in managing and showcasing properties online.
- Lack of technical knowledge to create and maintain a website.
- Limited visibility and reach to potential clients.
- Difficulty in communicating with clients and managing inquiries.
- Time-consuming process of updating property listings and information.

Goals

- Create a professional online presence to attract potential clients.
- Showcase properties and services offered by the network of brokers.
- Provide an easy way for clients to contact brokers and inquire about properties.
- Improve visibility and reach to potential clients through online marketing.
- Simplify the process of managing property listings and information.
