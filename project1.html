App for Recording Diabetes Symptoms

Description

The application is designed to help patients easily and effectively record their diabetes symptoms. It allows users to input information about their health status, facilitating the monitoring and management of their condition.

Objective

The main objective of the application is to provide an accessible tool for patients to record their symptoms and share this information with their doctors, thereby improving medical care and diabetes management.

Features

  • Easy daily symptom logging.
  • Intuitive and user-friendly interface.
  • Option to add additional notes.
  • Functionality to export data in PDF format.

Persona

User Story

Irene is an 80-year-old retiree who lives alone and has diabetes. She needs a reliable way to track her health symptoms to share during her regular doctor visits.

Age: 80 years
Gender: Female
Education: Bachelor's degree in Education
Location: Toluca, State of Mexico
Marital Status: Single
Occupation: Retired

Frustrations

- Lives alone and has no assistance with using devices.
- Frequently forgets to take measurements.
- Does not keep a reliable record to show her doctor.

Goals

- Consistently measure blood sugar and blood pressure levels.
- Record them in a consistent and reliable manner.
- Share this information with her doctor to receive appropriate treatment.
